ABOUT Us

Pasay, also known as the travel city, is one of the most populated cities in Metro Manila filled with busy markets, shopping malls and world-class art galleries. Located six kilometers from Manila and close to Makati, Taguig and Paranaque, Pasay is known for its amazing nightlife, adventures and shopping. This website will help and guide visitors to places they might have or haven't been in Pasay. Places they want to go, whether if it is shopping, eating, playing, or entertaining, Pasay To-Go will show them the places they will find interesting.
